With a reputation for being detail-oriented and innovative in problem-solving, I thrive in fast-paced, collaborative environments. I am particularly stoked about integrating my technical skills into the cutting-edge and futuristic realm of AI, especially focusing on orchestrating its convergence with tangible technological ecosystems. I have a profound enthusiasm for the convergence of disciplines like computer vision and machine learning, and I am eager to advance my career at the nexus of these transformative sectors.

But it's not just the theoretical knowledge & professional aspirations that define me. My skill set isn't just a collection of Coursera badges; it's a whole arsenal of hands-on experience and advanced know-how. I'm the kind of person who likes to question everything, tackling problems from physics's first principles approach to better understand the how and the why. In my free time, you'll catch me flipping through books on physics, unraveling the mysteries of neuroscience, mulling over philosophy, geeking out about rocket engines, and yes—of course nerding out to machine learning. It might sound like the typical '23-year-old engineer' spiel, but these aren't just hobbies to me; they're the pathways to innovation and the lenses through which I see potential in every challenge.
